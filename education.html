UiTM Kota Bharu

UiTMCK has ever since soared into a premier tertiary education centre and geared up by the establishment of its city campus (Kampus Kota) in Kota Bharu, in 2007. UiTMCK continues to strive and is ever committed into making UiTM a world class university at large.

2020-2023

UiTM Machang

Established on the 1st of July 1985 Universiti Teknologi MARA Cawangan Kelantan (UiTMCK) was established at Kem Kijang. In 1996, UITM Kelantan was relocated to the current permanent campus at Bukit Ilmu, Machang district.

2018-2019

MRSM Pengkalan Chepa

MRSM Pengkalan Chepa (originally MRSM Kota Bharu) is the 2nd MRSM established in 1973 after the establishment of MRSM Seremban (MARA Seremban College) in 1971. It was inaugurated on 12 August 1975 by YMM Almarhum Sultan Ismail Petra Ibni Almarhum Sultan Yahya Petra.
